The Value of Your Closet

Did you know there are over 2 trillion dollars of valuable unused clothing sitting in women’s closets? At Karen’s Pink Book, clients can buy, sell, trade and donate clothing and accessories for a cause, a cure, cash or new clothes for their fair pink book value.

What’s the pink book value? It’s the true value of clothing based on the resale value. Right now valuating clothing is all over the place. There are no transparent industry standards.

Karen’s Pink Book is to clothing, what Kelly’s Blue Book is to cars. When people begin to think about clothing as the investment that it really is, they won’t leave clothes hanging in their closets depreciating in value. Just like once we are done with a car. What do we do? We sell it, or we trade it in! I have a lifetime of experience buying, valuating and reselling used clothing.

For the customer at Karen’s Pink Book the process is transparent and incredibly simple. Customers consult karenspinkbook.com to have their clothing valuated and receive their postage paid pink bag. Simply toss the clothes in the bag and receive a tax deductible donation, cash, or new clothes. At Karen’s Pink Book she can “Clean out her closet, not her wallet.”
